Raid Shooter

Space themed shoot 'em up where you must blast away unrelenting enemies before they destroy you.


Features


Gameplay

Who cares? Just play. 2 Joystick for better precision and aim at the target. It's also lightweight since it loads from the Internet.

Stats

Show the stats of the best score, level, the number of rounds you have played, the total numbers of enemies you have defeated, the numbers of rounds of bullets you fired and the powerups you have collected.

Triller

Fan of levels? Progress? Challenge seems like Raid Shooter is the game for you.

Webvium

Lightweight, Fast, Material and Full-Featured Android Web Browser.
